Who We Are

We are a cycle rental service dedicated to providing eco-friendly, affordable, and accessible transport options in Ahmedabad. Founded in 2020, our mission is to promote sustainable urban mobility and healthy living. Our cycles are conveniently stationed at 120 major bus stands across Ahmedabad, making them easy to rent and return for your daily commutes.

Our Vision

To make Ahmedabad a greener, healthier city by encouraging the use of bicycles as a primary mode of transport. Our initiative not only reduces traffic congestion but also contributes to environmental conservation.
